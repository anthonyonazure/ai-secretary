About Söhne

Söhne (Klim Type Foundry) is a paid font. It isn't on Google Fonts or any free CDN, so it can't be rendered live in this preview without a license. Visit klim.co.nz/buy/sohne for proper specimens — or look at GitHub, OpenAI, Notion, or Stripe in production.

Visual character: grotesque-derived like Inter and Geist, but with subtle humanist warmth — slightly more characterful at headings, comparable readability at small sizes. Premium feel.

Cost: ~$300+ for a multi-weight family + per-domain web licensing. Per-domain licensing affects the embed surfaces (HubSpot/Salesforce extension, Slack/Teams hub, public DSAR portal, marketing site) — needs accounting for.

Closest free comparable rendering: none truly — Inter and Geist are both grotesque-derived but lack Söhne's specific warmth. Don't substitute.
