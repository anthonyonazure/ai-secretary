Section B — Distinctive Visual Identity

Step 8 commitment: invest pre-launch in distinctive visual signature on three surfaces. Empty-state illustration system is flagged for designer work — HTML mockup would mislead. RecordingStatusPill and CitationChip variations below; pick one of each (or a combination) to lock in.

RecordingStatusPill — 3 takes

V1 · Pulse-dot
Conventional. Small red dot pulsing + label + timer.
Recording 12:04
Pros: instantly readable, familiar from Loom / Zoom / Voice Memos. Cons: looks like every other recording indicator. Won't differentiate the product in screenshots.
V2 · Inline waveform ★
Distinctive. Micro waveform animation IN the pill — recording state IS the waveform.
Recording 12:04
Pros: Voice-Memos-evocative, ambient, distinctive. The waveform IS the recording confirmation. Cons: more rendering work; needs to perform on mobile lock screens too.
V3 · Gradient ring
Subtle. Rotating gradient ring + center dot. Apple-recording-ring evocative.
Recording 12:04
Pros: subtler than pulse, calmer. Cons: rotating motion may conflict with reduced-motion mode; needs a reduced-motion fallback.

CitationChip — 3 takes

Sample sentence below each variant shows how it reads inline.

V1 · Generic chip
Conventional. Rectangular chip with timestamp inside.
14:32
The buyer raised pricing concerns mid-call 14:32. They expressed strong interest in audit-log capabilities 22:08.
Pros: clear, scannable. Cons: looks like any tag/badge in the product; competes visually with module badges.
V2 · Iconic with glyph ★
Distinctive. Speaker glyph + timestamp in pill shape.
14:32
The buyer raised pricing concerns mid-call 14:32. Audit-log capabilities 22:08.
Pros: distinguishes citations from any other badge in the product; the glyph signals "this is a quote you can listen to." Cons: more visual weight inline; risks looking cluttered if a paragraph has many citations.
V3 · Superscript ref
Restrained. Academic-style superscript number, dotted underline.
14:32
The buyer raised pricing concerns mid-call¹. They expressed strong interest in audit-log capabilities². Pre-contract SOC2 evidence requested³.
Pros: minimal, doesn't break reading flow, very Perplexity-academic. Cons: hides the timestamp inline; user has to hover/click to see when in the recording. Less glanceable.

First-receipt empty state — illustration system

Flagged for designer work. Custom illustrations require an actual illustrator and brand-design pass — HTML/CSS mockup would mislead about the visual outcome. Recommended deliverables: 3 hand-drawn illustration concepts for the sample-meeting library, 2 motion illustrations for the streaming-receipt skeleton states, 1 hero illustration for first-launch empty state. Hand off to designer in parallel with Step 9; lock direction before component build-out begins.
